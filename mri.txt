There is no special preparation for an MRI exam. You can eat normally and continue to take your prescribed medications. When you arrive at the Imaging Center, you will need to remove all metal objects that you may be carrying. This inludes watches, jewelry, coins, keys, plens eyeglasses, dentures and hairpins. Since the magnet will erase information on bank cards and credit cards, these should be stored in locked dressing room that will be provided for your personal belongings. You may be asked to change into a robe, and an MRI technologist will escort you ino the scanning room.
